Biography [Who is this fool?]

Sarah McDaniel is both a true Granite Stater and a true child of the 90s.
Born in the great state of New Hampshire in 1993, she began her undergraduate
studies at Tufts University in the fall of 2011. Sarah is currently pursuing
English, French, and Computer Science as her academic concentrations; her
particular intellectual interests lie in language, semantics, and literary theory.
Her passion for education reform developed after her experience as a teacher
intern at Breakthrough Manchester during the summer of 2012.

In her spare time, Sarah enjoys snowshoeing, long-distance running, dancing,
and talking. She always welcomes discussion, collaboration, and conversation.
